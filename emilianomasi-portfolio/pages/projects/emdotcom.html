DESCRIPTION

The main goal of that project is to show to people or organizations how I work, what are my artistic, creative, and development skills, and how much I’m able merge them all together. In that webpage you will find a brief presentation on who I am and what are my points of view on the technology field, a timeline showing the main events of my working life, and some useful links to know more about my everyday life. Summarizing, this is my interactive business card.

INVOLVEMENT IN THE PROJECT

The reasons behind this project are essentially focused in having a product able to communicate in deep who I am. First of all I decided to go through a one page website, because I wanted to stress the fact that this page is like a business card, and scrolling down the content, the visitor starts to know me. More it scrolls down, and more its picture of me gets enriched.

I believe that there are three different knowledge layers that you can get from my personal web page.

The first one is related to my visual taste, and my way to bundle the information in a very clear manner. A simple layout with effective use of colors. Each section (About me, Timeline, and Contacts) is spaced from the previous one by a breaking section used to enrich my definition as a creative engineer.

The second layer is related to my front-end development skills concerning HTML, CSS, and Javascript. In fact, the code in that project explains in a straightforward manner how I use the previous mentioned technologies.

The third and last layer is related to my back-end development skills concerning the MVC pattern, and the creation of a modularized REST webservice.
